MIT’s 24-hour hackathon gathers over 1,000 undergraduate students from around the world. It takes place on the MIT campus. Students work on new software or hardware projects in education, new frontiers, entertainment, or sustainability. HackMIT hosts workshops for beginners and also offers beginner prizes. A few of the hackathon’s sponsors include Citadel, Sonr, Quantco, and Meta.
