BLOOD DONATION CAMP 2022

Donate, Inspire, Save: Join the Blood Donation Camp

About Event

In remembrance and tribute to the real-life heroes who lost their lives on the black day of 26/11, ENTESA team, in collaboration with NSS (National Service Scheme), is organizing a Blood Donation Camp at JSPM's RSCOE Tathawade Campus.

We are proud to partner with Akshay Medical Trust's, Akshay Blood Bank, Hadapsar, Pune, renowned for their efforts in organizing Voluntary Blood Donation Camps. The blood bank has provided all the necessary arrangements for the camp, including cots, beds, mattresses, and materials.

At the Voluntary Blood Donation Camp, each donor will receive a Blood Donation card as a token of appreciation. Additionally, a concession coupon and certificate will be attached to acknowledge their noble act.

Join us in this meaningful event as we honor the spirit of our lost heroes and contribute to saving lives through blood donation. Together, let's make a difference and uphold the values of compassion and service.
